DISCOVER WAKEUP ZONE

WakeUp.zone is an exciting next generation social network built on top of the powerful blockchain-based and smart contracts driven Sun Core engine that safeguards four pillar values of data protection and privacy.

BLOCKCHAIN SOLUTION

Trusted solution.

Data ownership and privacy.

The SUN Ecosystem is designed as a hybrid between blockchain technology and ElasticSearch database. Private key is used for signing data ownership. Each access to data is registered on the blockchain and available in the public blockchain explorer.

Our philosophy

Privacy

HOW IT WORKS

Create account. Get 10€*.

Start sharing and we will share back.

User creates account on the WakeUp.zone, generates public and private keys and gets a digital wallet. The Sun Core system will award each new user with SUN tokens equal to 10€.
* 1€ will be automatically donated to the Philanthropy account (regulated by a smart contract)

HOW TO EARN

Profile yourself. Earn more money.

Share your interests and affinities with us and we will share back.

We are not profiling users according to their posts, comments and likes. We do not truck our users. Instead we are offering you a set of forms to fill in that will tell us about your interests & affinities. For each form submitted you get SUN tokens equal to 10€*.
* 1€ will be automatically donated to the Philanthropy account (regulated by a smart contract)

Whenever you are targeted by advertisers (according to the forms you have filled in), you will receive a share of the price that advertiser paid for the ad placement. If you want to earn even more, go to the ‘Ads only’ section, browse through ads and get paid for each ad watched.

Philanthropy account. Help the planet and others in need

In order to help the efforts of many activists and organisations that are fighting for environment protection or helping to improve the lives of indigenous communities, we created a philanthropy account. Every single transaction on our platform will donate small fees to this account. Once a year, projects of a philanthropic nature will compete for the funds from the philanthropy account and you will be the jury.
